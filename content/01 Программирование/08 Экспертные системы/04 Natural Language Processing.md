## 7. Natural Language Processing (NLP)

NLP is a branch of AI focused on analyzing, understanding, and generating human language.
- **Goal:** To enable human-computer interaction by teaching computers to understand the way humans learn and use language.
- **Challenge:** Language is ambiguous and context-dependent (e.g., the sentence "Baby swallows fly" has multiple meanings).
- **Subfields:**

Natural Language Understanding
- Analysis of Language to Provide Meaningful Representation
- Natural Language Generation
- Production of Language from Representation

- **Steps in NLP:**

**Speech Recognition:** Analyzes an analog speech signal and extracts a sequence of words.
- **Syntactic Analysis:** Defines how words are grouped in a sentence. This is difficult in languages where word order doesn't solely determine function.
- **Semantic Analysis:** Extracts the meaning of a sentence after it has been syntactically analyzed, creating a representation of objects, relationships, and attributes.
- **Pragmatic Analysis:** Further clarifies the purpose of the sentence and removes remaining ambiguities.
