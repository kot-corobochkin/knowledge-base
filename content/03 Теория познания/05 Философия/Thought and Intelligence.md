### 4. Intelligence and Thought

- **Intelligence:** The ability to reason, develop new thoughts, perceive, and learn. There is no single, universally accepted definition.
    
- **Thought:** Defined as a mechanism that:
    
    - **Stimulates:** Action, information generation, and knowledge generation.
        
    - **Is triggered by:** External or internal stimuli.
        
    - **Acts through:** The present environment and past memory.
        
    - **Is stored as:** Neuronal states or electromagnetic thought waves.
