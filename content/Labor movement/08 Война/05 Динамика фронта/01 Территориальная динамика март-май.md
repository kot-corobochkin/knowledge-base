## 2.2. Ukrainian Counteroffensive Gains

Ukrainian forces have liberated **more than 430–440 km²** since January 29, primarily in the **Dnipropetrovsk–Zaporizhzhia administrative border area**. Ukrainian troops are now conducting clearing operations in northern Zaporizhzhia Oblast.
