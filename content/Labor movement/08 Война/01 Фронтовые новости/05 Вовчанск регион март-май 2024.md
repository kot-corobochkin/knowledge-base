## 4. Vovchansk Sector: Detailed Analysis

## 4.1. Background

Russia launched its offensive toward Vovchansk in **May 2024**. After nearly two years, Russian control in this sector amounts to:
- Vovchansk area: **140 km²**
- North toward Lyptsi: **83 km²**
- **Total: 223 km²** over almost two years — a modest gain reflecting slow progress.

## 4.2. Current Situation

Russian forces **do not control** the southeastern part of Vovchansk. They are attempting to advance along the **left bank of the Siverskyi Donets River** toward Semenivka, Shestaryvka, and Verkhnya Pysarivka.

## 4.3. Russian Tactical Problem

To reach Semenivka, Russian forces are stretched along a **4 km supply line** from their secured zones. This "intestinal" formation is vulnerable to being cut off by Ukrainian strikes. Russia is committing units from the **128th Motorized Rifle Brigade** to this push, but they are being neutralized by Ukrainian forces before securing footholds.
