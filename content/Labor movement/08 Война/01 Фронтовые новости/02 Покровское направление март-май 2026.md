## 3.1. Udachne Pocket (Pokrovsk Sector)

Russian forces closed a tactical pocket of approximately **2 km²** near Udachne. However, this operation began on **February 2** and took **over a month** of continuous fighting to complete.

## 3.2. Context of the Pokrovsk Sector

Despite having a massive **170,000-strong grouping**—the highest concentration of troops along the entire front—Russia's gains remain minimal. Their recent successes consist of closing small pockets, gaining only **1.5–2 km** over several weeks of intense combat.
