## 1. Latest Announcement: MMK

On Friday, the management of **Magnitogorsk Iron and Steel Works (MMK)** announced a 10% reduction in its managerial staff. CEO Pavel Shilyaev cited:
- A drop in plant utilization from 70–80% in 2025 to 60% in early 2026.
- External factors: geopolitical events, sanctions pressure, and global economic slowdown.
- A downturn across all industrial sectors, particularly construction, machinery, and oil &amp; gas.
Unused production units will also be taken offline.
