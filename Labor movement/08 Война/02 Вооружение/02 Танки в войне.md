## 2. The Changing Nature of Modern Warfare

## 2.1. Tanks Are No Longer Decisive

Massive tank assaults are a thing of the past. According to Oleksandr Kovalenko: "A tank in modern conditions is fire support for infantry, essentially ersatz artillery. We almost never see tank duels anymore." Without adaptation to drone warfare, even the most modern tank becomes a vulnerable target against FPV drone units.
