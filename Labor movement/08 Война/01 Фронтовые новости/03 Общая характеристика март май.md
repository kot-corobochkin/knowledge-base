## 2. Ukrainian Counterattacks and Liberated Territories
- **Operation Timeline:** Ukrainian counteroffensive operations began in late January of this year.
- **Territory Liberated:** Ukrainian forces have regained control over **more than 400 km²** of territory during this period.
- **Dnipropetrovsk Region:** The vast majority of this territory is in Dnipropetrovsk Oblast, which has been **almost completely liberated**. Only a few settlements remain to be cleared.
- **Key Units:** Air assault forces have been particularly active, restoring control over more than **285 km²** in March alone, according to Commander-in-Chief Syrskyi.
