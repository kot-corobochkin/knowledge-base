## 4. Russian Advances in Donetsk Oblast Toward Dnipropetrovshchyna

Russian forces have recently made tactical advances in the following settlements:
- Zeleny Hai
- Tovste
- Pidubne
- Namirne (direction)

These advances represent an attempt to **push from Donetsk Oblast westward into Dnipropetrovshchyna**, creating pressure from the east rather than only from the south (Zaporizhzhia axis).

**Key Observation:** These gains came **two to three months later** than anticipated, suggesting Russian operational delays. Moreover, even when Russian forces capture positions in this area, they **often lose them again**, indicating instability in their control.
