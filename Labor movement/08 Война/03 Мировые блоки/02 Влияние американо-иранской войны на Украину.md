## 5.1. Investment in Ukraine's Defense Industry

The conflict in the Middle East has highlighted vulnerabilities in regional air defense systems. This has drawn attention to **Ukrainian interceptor drones** as a cost-effective alternative to expensive missiles. Ukraine is now sending specialists and interceptor drones to Middle Eastern countries, which could lead to:
- Direct investments in Ukraine's defense-industrial complex (OPK)
- Technology exchanges
- Localization of production using Middle Eastern manufacturing facilities

## 5.2. Expanding Export Capabilities

Ukrainian weapons have proven their effectiveness in real combat. The emerging market interest from the Middle East opens new export opportunities, allowing Ukraine to **monetize its expertise** in countering threats like the Shahed-136.
